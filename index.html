About Me

Stockholm, Sweden Vehicle Engineer | Mechanical Engineer

I am a vehicle engineer based in Sweden, with focus on vehicle dynamics and vehicle controls. I specialize in modelling, simulation, and control of complex vehicle systems.

I am currently pursuing my master thesis on LPV and H-infinity control strategies for active and semi-active suspension systems, targeting improved chassis control and tyre load stability under varying operating conditions. My work involves using vehicle models, performing state-space analysis, and designing control strategies to improve ride behaviour, stability, and tyre road interaction.

Prior to my master’s, I worked as an Engineer in Strategy and Business Development at Doosan Bobcat, supporting product development and business strategy through data-driven insights derived from market and competitive analysis.

Origin Chennai, India : The city that is shaped by automotive and mechanical industries.
