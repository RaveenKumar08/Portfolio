About Me

Stockholm, Sweden Vehicle Engineer | Mechanical Engineer

I am an automotive engineer, with focus on vehicle dynamics and vehicle controls. I specialize in modelling, simulation, and control of complex vehicle systems.

I am currently pursuing my master thesis on LPV and H-infinity control strategies for active and semi-active suspension systems, targeting improved chassis control and tyre load stability under varying operating conditions. My thesis work involves using vehicle models, performing eigen mode analysis, and designing control strategies to improve ride behaviour, stability, and tyre road interaction.

Prior to my master’s, I worked as an Engineer in Strategy and Business Development at Doosan Bobcat, supporting product development and business strategy through data-driven insights derived from market and competitive analysis.
